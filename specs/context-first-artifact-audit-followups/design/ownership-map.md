# Ownership Map

## Artifact Ownership To Preserve

- `AGENTS.md`: repository-wide contract and hard invariants.
- `docs/spec-first-workflow.md`: detailed workflow mechanics and artifact shape guidance.
- Session skills: phase-specific protocol, allowed writes, stop rules, and handoff reminders.
- Deeper skills and references: compact behavior-change guidance, not alternate workflow authority.
- `spec.md`: task-local decisions for this follow-up.
- `design/`: task-local technical context for this follow-up.
- `tasks.md`: executable docs/skills ledger and proof expectations for this follow-up.

## Boundaries

- Do not move final decisions into workflow-control files.
- Do not make `design/overview.md` a planning ledger.
- Do not make `tasks.md` a second spec or design bundle.
- Do not make historical `specs/` bundles into universal templates.
